Why MediVault?

MediVault empowers patients to securely manage their health records and share them with trusted doctors. Inspired by DigiLocker, our platform ensures only verified professionals can edit records, while AI delivers personalized insights and multilingual support.

MediVault uses AI to analyze your medical records and provide personalized health insights, including trends and alerts.
Can I specify dietary restrictions or allergies?
Yes, you can input dietary restrictions or allergies, and MediVault will tailor health recommendations accordingly.
Is MediVault free to use?
MediVault offers a free tier with limited features, with premium plans available for advanced functionality.
Can I save or share my health records?
Yes, you can securely save and share your health records with authorized doctors through MediVault.
